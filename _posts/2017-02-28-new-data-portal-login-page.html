The City of Chicago Data Portal login page recently got a new look, along with other Socrata sites.  Users need not do anything different but since the change could prompt concerns about the legitimacy of the page, we wanted to verify the change.

Should you ever have concerns about the legitimacy of a page or anything else related to our site, we encourage you to reach out to us at dataportal@cityofchicago.org or @ChicagoCDO.  You can also contact Socrata directly through its support site.
