Due to an error in this morning’s update job, most records in the Crimes - 2001 to present dataset were deleted, leaving only 722 records. We are working on the problem and hope to have a complete dataset by tomorrow morning.

In the meantime, the Crimes - One year prior to present dataset, which shows a trailing year of records, appears to be accurate and can be used as an alternate source for that time period.
